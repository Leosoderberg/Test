About Me

I'm a UX Design student at IT-Högskolan, Gothenburg.

Coming from a teaching background, I have a knack for explaining things. My passion lies in design, and my goal is to create content that users can intuitively understand without needing to pause and think. I aim for streamlined, simplified, and personalized experiences.

I'm committed to making a positive impact, whether through student councils, class committees, volunteer organizations, or wherever I see an opportunity to make a difference.

People often describe me as positive, creative, and solution-driven. When I'm not immersed in Figma files, I enjoy helping colleagues and classmates. On the flip side, I'm an outdoor enthusiast, relishing walks, skiing, and exploring new locations.
